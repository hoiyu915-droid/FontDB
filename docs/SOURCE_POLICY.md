# Source and license policy

## Required evidence for a production font

- official family name and foundry/author
- canonical download or project URL
- exact license identifier and license text URL
- commercial-use status
- modification and redistribution conditions
- Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Latin, numeral, and punctuation coverage
- available weights/styles and variable-font axes
- last verification date

## Confidence states

- **reference_only**: visual sample exists; real font identity is unknown.
- **identified**: family appears identified; source or license incomplete.
- **verified**: source, license, and coverage checked.
- **tested**: verified plus layout and glyph stress tests passed.

## Reference-image rule

A montage, generated sample, marketplace preview, screenshot, or social post may establish visual intent only. It cannot establish the font name, authorship, license, or redistributability.

## Repository policy

Do not commit font binaries unless redistribution is explicitly permitted and necessary. Prefer canonical source URLs, checksums, and automated acquisition instructions. Record uncertainty instead of filling gaps by analogy.
